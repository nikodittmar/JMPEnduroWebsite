THE RACE

Come join us on August 6, 2022 for a one of a kind race experience. The Joaquin Miller Park Enduro will be a one-day event with three stages that include the best of what the park has to offer. Registration opens soon and will cost $100.

A Fundraiser For East Bay Bike Trails

All race proceeds will be donated to the Bicycle Trails Council of the East Bay (BTCEB). BTCEB is a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit organization founded in 1987 with the goal of educating trail builders, promoting responsible riding, and increasing trail access. BTCEB is the oldest mountain bike advocacy group in the United States and since its founding, members of the organization have worked countless hours to build and improve trails all over the East Bay!

THE COURSE

The Joaquin Miller Park Enduro will include three timed stages: Cinderella Trail, Chaparral Trail, and Castle Park Trail. Between each stage is a 3 mile climb with 500 feet of elevation gain. Pre-riding the course is strongly recommended. Videos of each trail are linked below:
